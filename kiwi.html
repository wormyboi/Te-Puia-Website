Kiwi Conservation Center

With a tempurature controlled environment, soundproof panels, special lighting for growing vegetation, and an isolation room for sick / injured kiwi, the kiwi conservation center at Te Puia is an award winning facility custom made to maximise the safety and well-being of the kiwi housed in it.

Kiwi Encounters

One of the greatest attractions at Te Puia is their kiwi conservation centre, where you get the once in a lifetime opportiunity to witness live kiwi in their custom built enclosure.

To ensure the well-being of the kiwi, no photography or videography is allowed inside the kiwi house. Mobile phones are also to be silenced or turned off, as bright lights can affect the kiwi.
