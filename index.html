Te Puia - New Zealand's Geothermal Wonderland

Te Puia is home to a number of attractions including Pōhutu Geyser, the largest active geyser in New Zealand, the New Zealand Māori Arts and Crafts Institute, and state a state of the art kiwi conservation centre. For visitor, Te Puia offers guided tours in which you can view all these attractions for yourself.

To learn more about Te Puia's guided tours, and how to book one, visit Te Puia's official website here.

Location

Te Puia is located at 20 Hemo Road, Tihiotonga, Rotorua 3040
