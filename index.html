Te Puia - New Zealand's Geothermal Wonderland

Te Puia is home to Pōhutu Geyser, the largest active geyser in New Zealand, as well as the New Zealand Māori Arts and Crafts Institute. At Te Puia, you can go on one of its guided tours, where you can view its geothermal valley, visit the national carving and weaving schools, and witness kiwi in its kiwi conservation center.

Location

Te Puia is located at 20 Hemo Road, Tihiotonga, Rotorua 3040
