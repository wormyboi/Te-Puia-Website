Āhua Gallery

The Āhua Gallery in Te Puia displays the artworks created by students at Te Puia's carving and weaving schools. Here, visitors are able to browse and purchase these artworks. This gallery allows visitors to see the result of the works of the students at Te Puia, while also promoting Māori culture to a global audience. Te Āhua Gallery can be viewed at the end of a guided tour or items for sale can be viewed on Te Puia's website here .
